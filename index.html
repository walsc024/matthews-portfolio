About Me


I am 25 years old and I pride myself on my hard work, integrity and positivity. Having worked at the busiest bar on Walt Disney property, worldwide, to a banana farm in the northern tropics of Australia, I know very well what hard work feels like and I am not afraid of it.

Entrepreneurship has been my driving force from a young age, this paired with my work ethic has always helped me learn and develop in every position I have obtained. Starting my own business at 17 gave me first hand experience of the ‘real world’ and I have been seeking new challenges and opportunities ever since.

Although considerably varied, I believe all of my life experiences are invaluable for any future employment. I am a firm believer in ‘learn by doing’ and feel my adaptability and overall character traits are skills you cannot learn in a classroom.
